# Research Quality

Use research only when it improves correctness or specificity.

## Source Priority

Prefer:

1. Primary sources, official documentation, papers, standards, datasets, or direct project pages.
2. Reputable secondary sources when primary sources are unavailable or too dense.
3. Blog posts or commentary only for interpretation, examples, or community context.

## Research Notes

Write `research.md` with:

```markdown
# Research Notes

## Claims Used

- Claim:
  Source:
  Confidence:
  Notes:

## Sources Checked

- Title:
  URL:
  Why it matters:
```

## Planning Rules

- Do not overload a 10-40 second scene with too many claims.
- Prefer one strong claim with a clear visual explanation.
- Flag uncertainty instead of hiding it.
- For current or unstable facts, verify sources before writing the plan.
- Keep citations in `research.md`; keep `scene_plan.md` focused on the video.
